Her story

From the banking hall to the digital frontier

Ruth's foundation was built on the front line of banking customer service, where she learned that real success comes from how you make people feel. That obsession with service, clarity, empathy, follow-through, now runs through everything she teaches.

Today she's a certified trainer within the Women in Digital Business programme (an initiative of ITC-ILO with Microsoft), where she completed the Training of Lead Trainers and earned her Microsoft badge in Artificial Intelligence for Women Entrepreneurs. She's on a mission to put practical AI and digital skills into the hands of women and young people, so they can build, sell and grow with confidence.

"Technology should feel like a friend, not a wall. I help you cross it, and then run.", Ruth
